Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information in our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Key Point: We do not collect, use, or share any personal data from our users. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Data Collection

Our application does not collect any personal information or usage data. All your data, including:

  • Your workout records and statistics
  • Personal preferences and settings
  • Any other information you enter

remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems. We do not have access to this data, and it is not synchronized with any external servers unless you choose to use iCloud backup (which is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure).

3. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any similar technologies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we do not collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your data through your device's settings and the app's built-in features.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please feel free to contact us:

support@yourmail.com
