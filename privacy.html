Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information in our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Key Point: We do not collect any personal data from our users. We only use analytics to understand how the app is being used (screen visits). Your privacy is our priority.

1. Data Collection

Our application does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information.

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. This includes:

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • General app usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crashes and performance data

This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics. Firebase Analytics may collect device identifiers and general usage information, but no personal data is collected or shared.

We do not use advertising services or tracking technologies for marketing purposes. We do not use cookies in our mobile application.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

3. Age Rating

This app is rated 9+ due to infrequent images of athletes in athletic poses that may be considered mildly suggestive. The content is purely educational and sports-related, with no explicit or inappropriate material.

4. Data Retention and Security

Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to delete or export.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please feel free to contact us:

dmapps.info@yahoo.com
